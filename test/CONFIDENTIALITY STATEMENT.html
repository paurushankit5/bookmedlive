At Bookmediz, we place a major emphasis on maintaining confidentiality of Customers information and Information Security Management. The following are key features of our Confidentiality Agreement, applicable to all our customers.

i) We do not disclose any information pertaining to our Customers to any third parties.

ii) We do not use our Customers identity for our marketing purposes, without their written consent.

iii) We never provide our Customer's name or Customer's information to other prospective Customers for marketing purposes.

iv) We do not sell or disclose our Customer list or Customer Information to third parties.

CONFIDENTIALITY AGREEMENT

Since it is a Hospitality Industry it is understood and agreed to that a Bookmediz Customer may provide certain information that is and must be kept confidential. To ensure the protection of such information, and to preserve any confidentiality necessary under patent and/or trade secret laws, it is agreed that:

1. The Confidential Information to be disclosed can be described as and includes personal information, health records, health data, doctor’s visit details, booking details, appointment details, medical history, test reports, payment information, regardless of whether such information is designated as "Confidential Information" at the time of its disclosure. This confidentiality statement is applicable for all our affiliates.

2. Bookmediz shall limit disclosure of Confidential Information within its own organization to its directors, officers, partners, members, employees, doctors, medical vendors and/or independent contractors (collectively referred to as "affiliates") having a need to know. Bookmediz and affiliates will not disclose the confidential information obtained from the discloser unless required to do so by law.

3. This Agreement imposes no obligation upon Bookmediz with respect to any Confidential Information

(a) that was in Bookmediz possession before receipt from Bookmediz Customer;

(b) is or becomes a matter of public knowledge through no fault of Bookmediz;

(c) is rightfully received by Bookmediz from a third party not owing a duty of confidentiality to the Bookmediz Customer;

(d) is disclosed without a duty of confidentiality to a third party by, or with the authorization of Bookmediz;

(e) is public knowledge or the information is available in public domain; or

(f) is independently derived by Bookmediz.

4. This Agreement states the entire agreement between Bookmediz and Bookmediz Customer concerning the disclosure of Confidential Information. Any addition or modification to this Agreement must be made in writing and signed by the parties.
